Track a repair

You can use this service to track repairs to council properties.

If you want to report a repair in a communal area you can also use this service to check whether somebody else has already reported it.

Start now

Before you start

This service can only track repairs that have already been reported. Please use the report a repair online service if you want to report a new repair.

Emergency repairs

An emergency repair is when there's immediate danger to you or the structure of the building. In an emergency we'll make the situation safe; we may need to return another day to complete the full repair.

All emergency repairs can be reported 24 hours a day, 7 days a week by calling 0800 952 4444 or 020 7525 2600. You may experience longer waiting times due to a high volume of calls.
